# Privacy Policy for YouTube Speed Controller

**Last Updated:** April 21, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0.0

---

## 1. Introduction

**YouTube Speed Controller** ("we," "our," or "the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that allows users to control YouTube video playback speed from 0.1x to 16x. This Privacy Policy explains how our Extension handles information when you use it.

By installing and using YouTube Speed Controller, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. What This Extension Does

YouTube Speed Controller is a productivity tool that:

- Allows you to adjust YouTube video playback speed (0.1x to 16x)
- Provides keyboard shortcuts for quick speed adjustments
- Offers preset speed buttons for convenient access
- Remembers your last used speed setting
- Works on YouTube, YouTube Music, and YouTube Shorts

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 User Settings

The Extension stores only your preferences and settings locally on your device using Chrome's Storage API (`chrome.storage.sync`). This includes:

- Your preferred playback speed settings
- Speed preset configurations
- Any customization preferences you make within the Extension

### 3.2 Personal Information

**We do NOT collect any personal information.** This includes, but is not limited to:

- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Browsing history
- YouTube video viewing history
- IP addresses
- Device identifiers
- Authentication credentials
- Financial information

---

## 4. Where Information is Stored

All data is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage mechanism:

- **Storage Method:** Chrome Storage Sync API (`chrome.storage.sync`)
- **Location:** Your local browser storage (on your device)
- **Cloud Sync:** Settings may sync across your Chrome browsers if you are signed into Chrome with sync enabled, but this is controlled by your Chrome browser settings, not by our Extension

**Important:** We do not have access to any servers or databases. All data remains on your device.

---

## 5. Third-Party Sharing

**We do NOT share any information with third parties.**

Specifically:

## 6. Analytics and Tracking

**We do NOT use any analytics or tracking technologies.**

This means:

## 7. External Communications

**The Extension does NOT communicate with any external servers.**

- No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have any)
- No data is transmitted to third-party servers
- No background connections are made
- The Extension works entirely offline after installation

## 8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

YouTube Speed Controller is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under the age of 13.

Since we do not collect any personal information:

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
- We do not require users to create accounts or provide age information
- Parents and guardians can safely allow children to use this Extension

This Extension complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) because no personal information is collected from any users, including children.
